Our Mission

We aim to reduce the devastating impact of wildfires by turning cutting-edge space data into early warnings. Our team of dedicated researchers and engineers works tirelessly to develop technology that can predict wildfire risks before they become catastrophic events.

By combining satellite imagery, environmental data, and advanced AI algorithms, we provide actionable insights that help communities prepare and respond to potential wildfire threats.

How FlameWatch Works

Satellite Data

We collect and analyze data from NASA FIRMS, POWER, and WMS satellites to monitor environmental conditions in real-time.

AI Prediction

Our Random Forest algorithm is trained on historical and environmental data to predict wildfire risks with high accuracy.

Real-Time Insights

Our interactive frontend provides live updates and actionable insights to help communities respond quickly.

Why It Matters

Increasing Wildfire Threats

Climate change has led to more frequent and severe wildfires around the world. The need for early detection and prevention has never been more critical.

Need for Early Intervention

Early warning systems can significantly reduce the impact of wildfires, saving lives, protecting property, and preserving natural ecosystems.

Real-World Impact

FlameWatch is ready for real-world deployment. Once we receive the required permissions, it can be used directly to predict wildfires and help protect communities and ecosystems.
